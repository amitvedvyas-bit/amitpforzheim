Hart's Core Insight

Ownership =
Decision Rights

  • AI can help execute decisions
  • AI cannot determine who should have authority
  • Ownership and governance still matter

Residual decision rights cannot be coded into a contract — or an algorithm.

But wait…

AI changes the marginal cost on both sides of the equation — inside and outside the firm.

Will AI lower internal or external costs faster?

The Real Debate

Which costs fall faster?

AI lowers internal costs faster

  • Automates HR, accounting, logistics — managing large teams gets cheaper
  • AI schedulers & analytics make large-scale production more efficient in-house

AI lowers external costs faster

  • AI platforms reduce friction of outsourcing — hiring, supply chains, collaboration
  • Easier to have specialized networked firms than one expensive generalist

Both arguments are strong. The answer depends on context.

Discussion

If AI reduces transaction costs close to zero, will the dominant organization of the future be a 100,000-person corporation — or a network of AI-enabled microfirms coordinated through digital platforms?
